Diversify Your Music - 4-Screen App

This was the second major project of the course. Our objective was to create an app where the user would be able to navigate through at least 4 different screens. I decided to approach this by creating an app that will present the user with information about different musical genres and artists.


Avoid the Asteroids - Conditional App

This was the third major coding assignment of the class. Our objective was to create an app that used conditional statements. I wanted to revisit creating a game so I decided to take the requirement of a conditional statement and use it in a function to determine if a collision had occured between the user, represented by a rocketship, and the asteroid. The conditional statement would determine if this had occured and what the program would do as a result.
