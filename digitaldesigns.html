This is the completed digital design that my team did. This includes design elements that were created by other people in my group (note: the stars, the moon, and the buildings) Everytime you reset the embedded demo, you will get a unique digital design that shows a Night-time City Skyline. Our objective with this digital design assignment was to create a program that will display random and unique variations of a design every time it is run.



This is embedded demo showcases the design element that I was responsible for. I wrote code that enable the program to have a random sky color, cloud color, and cloud size and amount.
